The National Institute of Electronics & Information Technology (NIELIT) Imphal, formerly known as DOEACC Imphal, is a leading technical education and training institute located in Akampat, about 5 km from the heart of Imphal city. Established in 1989 under the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology, Government of India, NIELIT Imphal is dedicated to developing skilled professionals in electronics, IT, and communication technologies. Spread across a 20-acre campus, the institute boasts state-of-the-art facilities including lecture halls, computer and electronics labs, a mechanical workshop, a well-equipped library, hostel accommodations, and staff quarters. The institute offers a variety of programs ranging from diploma and degree courses such as BCA, MCA, MSc IT, and engineering diplomas to industry-relevant short-term certifications in areas like Big Data, Machine Learning, and Office Automation. Many of these courses are affiliated with Manipur University and recognized by AICTE. To support students from disadvantaged backgrounds, NIELIT provides scholarships like the Protsahan Puraskar for SC/ST, physically handicapped, and female students. The institute also provides 100 Mbps high-speed internet through the National Knowledge Network and uses MOODLE for digital learning. NIELIT Imphal plays a crucial role in bridging the digital divide in the Northeast by nurturing talent through quality education and skill development.
